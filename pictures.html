2021 VIRTUAL CHALLENGE
This 2021 virtual event consisted of part of our robotics team working on creating a game field based on certain requirement s for robots to possibly play in during future years within FIRST. Our team received the engineering design award for this event and created a presentation as well. Most importantly, the team gained numerous important skills such as communication and CAD designing skills by effectively cerating a field setup based on the intial planning. After doing so, our team also created an animation and recorded a game introduction video to show to the judges.


The product is a mixed reality (AR and VR) headset with controllers. The headset will contain the user’s smartphone. The headset adds gameplay features into the physical world seen by the user. A heart rate sensor and calorie tracker allow the user to track the intensity of their workout sessions. The controllers will also provide haptic feedback to create a realistic touch experience. Additionally, the sensor and tracker send details to a smartphone application. The app calculates and displays exercise details and statistics, and the application will include multiplayer leaderboards, exercise streaks, and AI recommendations. The software for the headset will exist within the smartphone app.
